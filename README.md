## 🌾 Kisan Bandhu

Kisan Bandhu is a Smart Crop Advisory & Farmer Marketplace Platform designed to empower Indian farmers by connecting them directly with buyers and providing intelligent crop advisory solutions.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Indian farmers often face:

- ❌ Middlemen exploitation
- ❌ Lack of real-time crop advisory
- ❌ Unfair pricing
- ❌ Limited awareness of government schemes
- ❌ Poor market transparency

These issues reduce farmer income and growth opportunities.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Kisan Bandhu aims to solve these problems by:

- ✅ Direct Farmer-to-Buyer Marketplace
- ✅ Smart Crop Advisory System
- ✅ MSP Price Calculator
- ✅Subsidy Price calculator
- ✅ Government Scheme Awareness
- ✅ Transparent Digital Platform

---

## 🚜 Features
